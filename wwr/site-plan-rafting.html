Overview

Purpose

The purpose for this site is to help share information with potential clients, therefore also increasing revenue. Our rafting site should excite viewers enough to want to contact us to book a trip.

Audience

Our target audience should target adventurers, outdoor enthusiasts, families, and youth groups. This site should answer questions about pricing, and what we do different/better than other rafting companies. They should be able to access this site from desktop and mobile.
