Half Day Adventure

Join us for an exhilarating half-day rafting trip that combines thrill and natural beauty. Perfect for both beginners and seasoned adventurers, this trip takes you down a stunning stretch of river featuring Class II and III rapids. You'll experience the rush of the rapids, mixed in with calm sections where you can enjoy the breathtaking scenery and wildlife.

Our experienced guides will ensure your safety and provide engaging stories about the area's history and ecosystem. Whether you're looking for a quick adventure or an introduction to whitewater rafting, this half-day excursion promises excitement, fun, and unforgettable memories.

Trip Details:

Duration: Approximately 4 hours

Departure: 9:00 AM and 2:00 PM daily

Includes: All rafting gear, professional guide, and light refreshments

Book your spot today and embark on a thrilling half-day journey down the river with us!

Full Day Adventure

Embark on an unforgettable full-day rafting adventure that promises both adrenaline-pumping excitement and stunning moments of natural beauty. This trip covers an extensive stretch of river with a mix of Class III and IV rapids, offering a thrilling challenge for adventure seekers. Between the rapids, take in the stunning landscapes and enjoy the tranquility of the river's calmer sections.

Our knowledgeable guides will lead you through each rapid, ensuring your safety while sharing fascinating insights about the local history, geology, and wildlife. This full-day excursion includes a delicious riverside lunch, giving you time to relax and refuel before continuing the adventure.

Trip Details:

Duration: Approximately 8 hours

Departure: 8:00 AM

Includes: All rafting gear, professional guide, lunch and light refreshments

Don't miss out on this comprehensive rafting experience that will leave you with lasting memories and stories to share. Reserve your spot now and prepare for a day of ultimate river adventure!

Overnight Adventure

Immerse yourself in the ultimate rafting experience with our overnight adventure. This trip combines thrilling rapids, serene river stretches, and a night under the stars, creating a truly unique and memorable journey. You'll tackle Class III and IV rapids, guided by our experienced team who will ensure your safety and provide fascinating insights about the river and its surroundings.

As the day winds down, you'll set up camp along the riverbank, enjoying a freshly prepared dinner. Spend the evening around the campfire, sharing stories and soaking in the natural beauty. Wake up to the sounds of nature and a delicious breakfast before embarking on another exciting day on the river.

Trip Details:

Duration: 2 days, 1 night

Departure: 8:00 AM

Includes: All rafting gear, professional guide, all camping gear, all meals, and snacks

Join us for an extraordinary overnight rafting trip that combines adventure, relaxation, and the splendor of the great outdoors. Book your overnight rafting experience today and create lasting memories on the river!
